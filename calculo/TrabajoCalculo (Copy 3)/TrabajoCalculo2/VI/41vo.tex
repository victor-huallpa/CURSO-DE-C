$\displaystyle \int_{-2}^{2} \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4-x^2}}$

\nopagebreak
\begin{align*}
I &= \int_{-2}^{2} \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4-x^2}} = [\arcsin(x/2)]_{-2}^{2} \\
&= \arcsin(1) - \arcsin(-1) = \pi/2 - (-\pi/2) = \pi
\end{align*}
