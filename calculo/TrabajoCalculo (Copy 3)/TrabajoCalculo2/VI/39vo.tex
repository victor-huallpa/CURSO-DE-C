$\displaystyle \int_{-\infty}^{0} \frac{dx}{(4-x)^2}$

\nopagebreak
\begin{align*}
I &= \lim_{a \to -\infty} \int_{a}^{0} (4-x)^{-2} \, dx \\
&= \lim_{a \to -\infty} \left[ (4-x)^{-1} \right]_{a}^{0} \\
&= \lim_{a \to -\infty} (\frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{4-a}) = 1/4 - 0 = 1/4
\end{align*}

\[
\boxed{\displaystyle 1/4}
