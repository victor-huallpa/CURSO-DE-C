$\displaystyle \int_{-\infty}^{0} xe^x\,dx$

\nopagebreak
\begin{align*}
\text{Por partes: } u=x, dv=e^x dx \implies du=dx, v=e^x. \\
\int xe^x dx = xe^x - e^x. \\
I &= \lim_{a \to -\infty} [xe^x - e^x]_{a}^{0} \\
&= (0 - 1) - \lim_{a \to -\infty} (ae^a - e^a) \\
&= -1 - (0 - 0) = -1
\end{align*}

\[
\boxed{\displaystyle -1}
