$\displaystyle \int_{2}^{4}\frac{e^{\ln x}}{x^2+7}\,dx$

\nopagebreak
\begin{align*}
\text{Simplificamos el integrando: } e^{\ln x} = x. \\
I &= \int_{2}^{4} \frac{x}{x^2+7} \, dx \\
\text{Sustitución: } u = x^2+7 \implies du = 2x \, dx \implies x \, dx = du/2. \\
\text{Límites: } x=2 \to u=11; \quad x=4 \to u=23. \\
I &= \int_{11}^{23} \frac{1}{u} \frac{du}{2} = \frac{1}{2} [\ln|u|]_{11}^{23} \\
&= \frac{1}{2} (\ln 23 - \ln 11) = \frac{1}{2} \ln \frac{23}{11}
\end{align*}

\[
\boxed{\displaystyle \frac{1}{2} \ln \frac{23}{11}}
\]
