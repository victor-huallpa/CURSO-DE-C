$\displaystyle \mathbf{\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sin^{i}(ax)}$
\nopagebreak

Sea \(r=\sin(ax)\).

\[
r^{i+1}-r^{i}=r^{i}(r-1).
\]

Despejando:
\[
r^{i}=\frac{r^{i+1}-r^{i}}{r-1}.
\]

Sumando de \(i=1\) a \(n\):
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n} r^{i}
=
\frac{1}{r-1}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(r^{i+1}-r^{i}).
\]

La suma telescópica cumple:
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n}(r^{i+1}-r^{i})
= r^{n+1}-r.
\]

Sustituyendo \(r=\sin(ax)\):
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sin^{i}(ax)
=
\frac{\sin^{n+1}(ax)-\sin(ax)}{\sin(ax)-1}.
\]

Factorizando:
\[
\boxed{
\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sin^{i}(ax)
=
\frac{\sin(ax)\,\big(\sin^{n}(ax)-1\big)}{\sin(ax)-1}
}
\]
