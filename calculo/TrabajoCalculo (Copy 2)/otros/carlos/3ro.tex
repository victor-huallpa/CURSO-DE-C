$\displaystyle \mathbf{\sum_{i=1}^{n} (\sqrt{9+x})^{\,i}}$
\nopagebreak

Sea \(r=\sqrt{9+x}\).

\[
r^{i+1}-r^{i}=r^{i}(r-1).
\]

Despejando:
\[
r^{i}=\frac{r^{i+1}-r^{i}}{r-1}.
\]

Sumando de \(i=1\) a \(n\):
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n} r^{i}
=
\frac{1}{r-1}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(r^{i+1}-r^{i}).
\]

La suma telescópica cumple:
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n}(r^{i+1}-r^{i})
= r^{n+1}-r.
\]

Sustituyendo \(r=\sqrt{9+x}\):
\[
\sum_{i=1}^{n} (\sqrt{9+x})^{\,i}
=
\frac{(\sqrt{9+x})^{\,n+1}-\sqrt{9+x}}{\sqrt{9+x}-1}.
\]

Factorizando:
\[
\boxed{
\sum_{i=1}^{n} (\sqrt{9+x})^{\,i}
=
\frac{\sqrt{9+x}\,\big((\sqrt{9+x})^{\,n}-1\big)}{\sqrt{9+x}-1}
}
\]
